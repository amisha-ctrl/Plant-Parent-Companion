│   │   ├── Tulsi (Holy Basil)  
│   │   │   ├── Remedy: Tulsi Kadha  
│   │   │   │   ├── Ingredients: Tulsi leaves (10), Ginger (1-inch), Black Pepper (5-6), Honey (1 tsp), Water (2 cups)  
│   │   │   │   ├── Preparation: Boil Tulsi leaves, ginger, and black pepper in water until reduced to half. Strain and add honey.  
│   │   │   │   ├── Use: Treats cold, cough, fever, and boosts immunity.  
│   │   ├── Neem

│   │   │   │   ├── Preparation: Boil Neem leaves in water for 10 minutes, strain, and add honey and lemon juice.  
│   │   │   │   ├── Use: Detoxifies the body, treats skin infections, and helps in diabetes management.
